Design System — v1 · 2026.04.15

One system.
Four pages.
Consistent by design.

This page is the single source of truth for every token, component, and typographic decision that the Main, Portfolio, Hiring, and Blog pages will share. Nothing ships to the live site unless it can be expressed in the vocabulary below.

01 / Color

Black on white, with one accent reserved for HYPEER.

The master brand lives in pure black and white. Orange #EF4A24 appears only inside HYPEER-branded sections or pages, so that the color itself becomes a wayfinding signal.

02 / Typography

Bold sans for voice, serif for reading.

Inter (900) handles every headline so the brand echoes the KREATORS. wordmark. Newsreader carries long-form body copy, giving research-grade content the weight and rhythm of an editorial magazine. Pretendard covers Korean across the full system.

03 / Buttons

Three button types. No more.

Default (outline on white), Dark (filled black), and HYPEER (filled orange — used only inside HYPEER contexts). A right-pointing arrow appears on every primary call-to-action to reinforce forward motion.

Tags classify — they don't decorate.

Four variants in total. Outline (default), Solid (strong emphasis, rare), HYPEER Outline (for HYPEER-associated projects on the master site), HYPEER Solid (only inside HYPEER pages).

08 / Spacing & grid

8px base, 12 columns, 1440 max.

Section padding is 96px top/bottom on desktop. Horizontal page padding scales from 20px (mobile) to 56px (large desktop). The grid uses 12 columns with 24px gutters — never fewer.
